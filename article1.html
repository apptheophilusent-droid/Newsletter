Tech Leaders Gather in Accra

Published: September 23, 2025 · By NewsPulse Staff

Accra hosted its largest tech summit to date, bringing together innovators, investors, and policymakers from across Africa and beyond. The event focused on emerging trends in fintech, AI, and sustainable development.

Keynote speakers included founders of leading startups, government officials, and global tech executives. Panels explored how Ghana’s digital infrastructure is evolving and what it means for the future of entrepreneurship in West Africa.

“We’re seeing a shift where Accra is becoming a hub not just for local innovation, but for global collaboration,” said one panelist. The summit also featured live demos, startup pitches, and networking sessions that sparked new partnerships.

Organizers announced plans to expand the summit in 2026, with a focus on youth-led innovation and cross-border tech ecosystems.
